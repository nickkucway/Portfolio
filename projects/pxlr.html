Pirate's Blackjack

Pirate's blackjack is a simplified version of Blackjack, Pirate's are not very smart so they don't bog themselves down with all of the nuance of a typical blackjack game. Whoever can reach 21 first wins, but don't go over 21 or you will automatically lose.
